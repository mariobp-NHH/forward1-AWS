Forward: Connecting Education and Society

Forward is an educational platform that aims to connect education and society making that interaction fluent and effective.

To achieve that objective, Forward hosts different courses to facilitate the access to education by active learning. Forward also host thematic platforms where the users have access to dashboards that disclose useful data in different disciplines, and where the user have access to frontier research in those disciplines.
